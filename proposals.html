Project Proposals

FitMeal Planner

One-liner: A digital meal planning tool for people who want to eat healthier with less effort.

Problem & Audience: Many people struggle to plan balanced meals and often rely on fast food. FitMeal Planner targets busy students and professionals who want to save time and stay healthy.

Core Features (MVP):

  • Personalized weekly meal plans
  • Recipe suggestions with calorie info
  • Shopping list generator
  • Dietary preference filters
  • Simple, mobile-friendly design

SkinSense

One-liner: A smart web app that scans your skin with your camera and gives personalized care advice.

Problem & Audience: Skincare advice is often generic, leaving people unsure which products or routines fit their needs. SkinSense helps individuals who want tailored, science-based recommendations without visiting a dermatologist.

Core Features (MVP):

  • Access to user’s camera for live skin scan (with consent)
  • Detection of dryness, acne, redness, or early aging signs
  • Personalized skincare routine suggestions
  • Product recommendations based on results
  • Progress tracking with “skin diary” option
  • Simple reports with easy-to-read feedback
  • Future option: connect with dermatologists online

EventRadar

One-liner: A web app to discover events happening near you in real time.

Problem & Audience: People often miss interesting local events because information is scattered across platforms. EventRadar targets students and young professionals who want an easy way to find concerts, workshops, and meetups nearby.

Core Features (MVP):

  • Event discovery by location and date
  • Filters by interest (music, sports, tech, etc.)
  • Map-based event search
  • Save & share events with friends
  • Mobile-friendly UI
